Credit Card Marketplaces See Surge

This article discusses how credit card marketplaces have experienced increased demand as consumers look for ways to save money and manage their finances during the pandemic.

Many people have experienced job losses or reduced income due to the pandemic, making it difficult to make ends meet. As a result, more consumers are looking for credit cards that offer cash back, rewards, or low interest rates to help them stretch their budgets further. Credit card marketplaces can help users compare different credit card offers and find the one that best fits their needs.
